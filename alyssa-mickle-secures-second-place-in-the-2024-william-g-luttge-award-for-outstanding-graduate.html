Alyssa Mickle secures second place in the 2024 William G. Luttge Award for Outstanding Graduate

edale

Congratulations to Alyssa Mickle for securing second place in the 2024 William G. Luttge Award for Outstanding Graduate Research in Neuroscience. Her exceptional dedication, innovative approach, and substantial contributions have greatly advanced our comprehension of neuroscience's intricacies. We are excited to see her future accomplishments and the continued impact of her work in the neuroscience community!
