Caitlin Brennan's presentation at the NCF SfN

amickel

Caitlin Brennan's presentation at the NCF SfN focused on the distribution of c-Fos protein in the thoracic spinal cord after cervical spinal cord stimulation and injury. During this presentation Caitlin highlighted the impact of electrical stimulation on neural activity and recovery post-injury, offering insights into potential rehabilitation strategies for spinal cord injuries. Congratulations on your first meeting presentation!!
